The Company

A quiet standard for Libya's roads.

Alkhayar was founded in Tripoli in 1999 to be the trusted bridge between Korean manufacturers and the Libyan market — committed to importing Korean-made parts and lubricants the company stands behind, in full, with its name and reputation.

Today, we distribute to maintenance centers, auto parts retailers, and transport fleets across Libyan cities — from the independent workshop to the largest corporate fleets.

Two exclusive Korean agencies, three branches, and twenty-five years serving the Libyan market. A reputation built one order at a time.
